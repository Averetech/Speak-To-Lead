About

Speak To Lead. exists to tell you more about organizations that teach public speaking and leadership skills. These are great skills that are needed across different industries and I highly recommend anyone that would like to improve in them to join Toastmasters International. Toastmasters International is a non-profit organization that helps people develop communication, public speaking, and leadership skills through a worldwide network of clubs that meet online and in person.

Do you want to know more about Toastmaster International? Then look no further...

It has been a year since my first club meeting. After eight speeches and near-perfect monthly meeting attendance, I would like to say this is an amazing organization. Toastmasters brings a lot of value to someone new to public speaking and that is probably why it gained so much popularity online. The clubs offer an extremely friendly and safe environment for you to practice and fail, without having to worry about harsh judgement and career consequences. For you?
